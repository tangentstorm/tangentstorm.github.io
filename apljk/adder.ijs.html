1 a half adder

( ~:/~ ; *./~ ) 0 1   NB. sum is ~: (xor)   carry is *. (and)

┌───┬───┐
│0 1│0 0│
│1 0│0 1│
└───┴───┘

ha =: (~: , *.)"0
(0 ha 0); (0 ha 1); (1 ha 0); (1 ha 1)

< @ ha /~ 0 1

NB. compare to http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adder_(electronics)
A ,. B ,. C ,. S  NB. C=carry, S=sum for inputs A and B
